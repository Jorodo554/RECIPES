Recipe

  • 24 oz Marinara sauce, (3cups)

Preparation procedure

  1. Combine 1.5 cups of mozzarella
  2. 1/4 cup parmesan cheese,ricotta,parsley,egg and 1/4 teaspoon salt
  3. Spread 1 cup of the meat sauce in a 9*13 pan or casserole dish
    4. Top it with 3 lasagna noodles
  4. Layer with 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture and 1 cup of meat sauce
